# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Participants are expected to create a professional, inclusive, and technically rigorous environment in which ideas can be challenged without attacking people.

## Expected behavior

- Engage with evidence and reasoning.
- Distinguish disagreement from misconduct.
- Make room for implementor, governance, security, accessibility, and affected-party perspectives.
- Disclose relevant conflicts of interest.
- Respect privacy and coordinated security disclosure.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, and sustained disruption.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer. Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue warnings, restrict participation, or ban participants when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.
